Thesis Research
AI Use, Social Function, and Psychological Well-Being Among Youth
Examining patterns of generative AI use among ~ 3,000 adolescents to understand relationships between relational AI use, social connection, and psychological well-being.

An AI based well-being journaling app for software developers grounded in Self-Determination Theory, incorporating autonomy, competence, and relatedness metrics alongside energy and stress measures tied to daily check-in scores.
